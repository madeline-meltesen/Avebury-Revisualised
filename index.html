Welcome to Avebury Revisualised

This interactive map explores evolving interpretations of Avebury Henge (Wiltshire, England) through historic plans by antiquarians and archaeologists from the 17th–20th centuries. Use this map to:

  • Compare plans by John Aubrey (1626–97), William Stukeley (1687–1765), Alexander Keiller (1889–1955), and Isobel Smith (1912–2005); and
  • Customise views with adjustable colours and opacity for each layer
